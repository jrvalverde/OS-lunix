Cross Development System

A complete cross development system consisting of lupo a universal preprocessor for assembler sources, luna an assembler designed for creating LUnix applications, lld a linker for objectfiles created by luna. The whole system is written in C and will compile with GCC (GNU C-Compiler). There are compiled binarys for AMIGA, MS-DOS and OS/2.
