  • Jun 22 - Luna now calls lupo AND lld automatically if neccessary. This is another expansion to X-LULu. Default include-directorys and default librarys are now defined by environment variables. And there new items in the standard library: setenv, getenv and unsetenv. Calls that are used to handle environment variables under LUnix ! There also are brand new include files, that should be the new standard.
